As far as I know the escape characters are:
\n newline
\r carriage return
\t tab
\v vertical tab
\f form feed
\a alert (bell)
\b backspace
\0 null character
\\ backslash
\’ single quote (inside single quote)
\” double quote (inside double quote)
\? question mark (for pre-processor trigraphs)

see: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Escape_sequences_in_C
